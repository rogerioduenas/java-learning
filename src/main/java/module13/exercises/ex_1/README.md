## Detailed Description

A simple system needs to register products and allow querying their basic data, such as name and price.

This exercise focuses on introducing a base class and reinforcing the concept of encapsulation.

---

## Required Classes

### `Product`

#### Attributes
- `name`
- `price`

#### Methods
- Update the product price
- Retrieve the product data

---

## Business Rules

- The price **must not be accessed directly** outside the class
- The price **must always be a positive value**

---

## Expected Inputs

- Product name
- Initial price
- New price for update

---

## Expected Output

- Updated product data (name and price)

---

## Technical Goal

- Introduce a base class
- Reinforce encapsulation and validation of business rules
